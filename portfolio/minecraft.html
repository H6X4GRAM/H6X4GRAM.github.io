The Dildo

I built a giant dildo in Minecraft as part of an assignment to sculpt an object of cultural significance. I chose the dildo not only for the shock factor, but that I also believe that is turly an onject of cultural significance. The dildo is the penis without the man, an object that can pleasure many without needing the energy to deal with the commitment and needs of another man.

3d Printing

The Dildo was 3D printed using the Flashforge Adventurer 4. The final version of the print stand approximately 10 inches tall. Without the testicles, the penis is 7 inches in length.
