MEME MASHUP

Fallen Angel

This meme mashup was made by combining the painting, Grande Odalisque, with two memes: Nigel Thornberry, and man laughing in the dark.

I pasted Thornberry directly on top of the face and removed the hair and ear. I changed the hue to match the painting and even added drop shadow and brushed some shade and higlights to add more depth to the face. I also added a noise filter to make the image seem less flat.
